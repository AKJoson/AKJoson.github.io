FlashGoPro Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Welcome to FlashGoPro ("we", "our", "the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

FlashGoPro is a flashlight utility app. We collect minimal information necessary for the App to function:

  • Camera Access: The App requests camera permission solely to control the device's flashlight (torch). We do not capture, store, or transmit any photos or videos.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • App Tracking Transparency: On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You may choose to allow or deny this request.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To operate the flashlight functionality of the App
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: Used for advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple SKAdNetwork: Used for ad attribution without revealing personal data.

4. Data Storage and Security

FlashGoPro does not store any personal data on our servers. All App settings (brightness, mode preferences) are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit any personal information to external servers beyond what is required by our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob).

5. Children's Privacy

FlashGoPro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny tracking permission when prompted by the App Tracking Transparency dialog
  • Revoke camera permission at any time through iOS Settings
  • Reset your advertising identifier through iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your use of the App

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
