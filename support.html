Thank you for using LAN File & Photo Sharing Tool.

This app helps you efficiently share photos, videos, and files over a local network. No registration or cloud upload is required, ensuring your privacy and data security.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 📱 How to connect devices? Make sure all devices are on the same Wi-Fi network and scan the QR code provided by the app.
  • 🧾 Do I need to log in? No login is required. The app works completely offline within your local network.
  • 🗂️ Is the file transfer encrypted? All transfers are limited to your LAN and are not accessible externally.
